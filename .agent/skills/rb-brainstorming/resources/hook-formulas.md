# Hook Formulas for Home Remodeling

Proven headline formulas adapted for remodeling marketing.

---

## Formula Categories

### 1. Direct Response (Problem → Solution)

**Formula:** `[Pain Point]? [Solution] in [Timeframe].`

- "Hate your outdated kitchen? Get a stunning remodel quote in 24 hours."
- "Embarrassed to host? Transform your space in 3 weeks."
- "Tired of fighting for bathroom time? Add a second bath for less than you think."

---

### 2. Curiosity / Intrigue

**Formula:** `The [Unexpected Truth] about [Topic]`

- "The #1 mistake homeowners make when hiring a contractor"
- "Why most kitchen remodels go over budget (and how to avoid it)"
- "What your neighbors aren't telling you about their renovation"

---

### 3. Social Proof / Story

**Formula:** `How [Person Like Them] got [Result]`

- "How the Martinez family got their dream kitchen without moving out"
- "See how we transformed this 1970s bathroom in just 10 days"
- "This homeowner saved $15K on their remodel. Here's how."

---

### 4. Offer-Led (For Cold Traffic)

**Formula:** `Get [Specific Benefit] for [Reduced Cost/Effort]`

- "Get a luxury kitchen for $350/month — financing available"
- "Free 3D design consultation (normally $500)"
- "Book this month, save 15% on materials"

---

### 5. Urgency / Scarcity

**Formula:** `[Limited Availability] + [Reason Why]`

- "Only 3 project slots left this quarter"
- "Spring booking special ends Friday"
- "Lock in 2025 pricing before materials increase"

---

### 6. Before/After (Visual Hooks)

**Formula:** `[Before State] → [After State]`

- "Cramped → Chef's Paradise"
- "Dated → Modern Farmhouse"
- "Dysfunctional → Dream Bath"

---

## Remodeling-Specific Hooks (Swipe File)

### Kitchen
- "What if you actually loved cooking again?"
- "Stop apologizing for your kitchen."
- "Finally, a kitchen that works as hard as you do."

### Bathroom
- "Your bathroom should feel like a spa, not a chore."
- "Two words: heated floors."
- "The bathroom you deserve after a long day."

### Whole Home
- "Fall in love with your home again — without moving."
- "Why move? Remodel."
- "The home you imagined when you bought it."

---

## Usage

1. Pick a formula that matches your campaign goal
2. Fill in with client-specific details
3. Test 2-3 variations in ads
4. Double down on winners
