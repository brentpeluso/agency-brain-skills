- **Visual Identity:** Trusted, Professional, Growth-Oriented.
- **Logo Concepts:** House (Construction) + Check/Arrow (Results/Booking).

- **Authenticity:** Real job sites, messy-to-clean transitions.
- **Logo Usage:**

    - White version (reversed) on Navy backgrounds.
