# Grand Slam Offer Copy Framework (Remodeler Edition)

## The Value Equation
```
Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) ÷ (Time Delay × Effort)
```

## The Four Levers

### 1. Dream Outcome
**Bad:** "We do renovations."
**Good:** "Get the Open-Concept Chef's Kitchen You've Always Wanted."

Keywords: Luxury, Space, Comfort, Home Value, Envied by Neighbors

### 2. Perceived Likelihood
- Proof: "50+ 5-Star Google Reviews"
- Authority: "Licensed, Bonded & Insured since 2010"
- Guarantee: "On time, or we pay you $100/day"

### 3. Time Delay
- "Quote in 24 hours"
- "Most bathrooms done in under 2 weeks"

### 4. Effort & Sacrifice
- "We handle all permits and HOA approvals"
- "Daily cleanup. No mess left behind."

## Headline Formula
```
[Target Audience] + [Dream Outcome] + [Without Pain]
```
**Example:** "Homeowners in [City]: Update Your Kitchen In 14 Days Without Living in Chaos."

## The Offer Stack
Name the mechanism, then stack value:
```
The 14-Day Rapid Remodel System™
├── Free In-Home Consultation ($500 Value)
├── 3D Design Render ($1,000 Value)
└── Material Selection Guide ($200 Value)

CTA: "Claim Your Free Estimate Now"
```

## Remodeler Hooks (Swipe File)
- "Tired of your cramped kitchen?"
- "Embarrassed to host dinner parties?"
- "Need a bathroom that feels like a spa?"
- "Your home's value is stuck because of [outdated room]."
- "Hate your kitchen? You'll love what we can do in 14 days."

# Hormozi Skills Pack (Tactical Blueprints)

Use these blueprints when defining **execution steps** for new skills.

## Global Variables
Define these for the client context:
```
{{avatar}} = Homeowner in [City] needing remodel
{{pain}} = Outdated, cramped, embarrassed, low home value
{{dream_outcome}} = Luxury, pride, functionality, increased equity
{{PLA_proof}} = Before/Afters, [X] 5-star reviews, License #
{{time_delay}} = Quote in 24h, Done in [X] weeks
{{effort_sacrifice}} = Dust, noise, permits, contractors disappearing
{{lead_magnet}} = Free Quote, Design Consultation, Pricing Guide
```

## Blueprint 0: Value Equation Builder
**Goal:** Maximize value in one stack.
> **Prompt:** "Using the variables, write a 5-line value stack:
> 1. Dream Outcome in avatar’s words.
> 2. Proof: 3 bullets + Guarantee.
> 3. Speed: First win in [Hours] + Full outcome in [Weeks].
> 4. Ease: 3 'You don't have to' bullets.
> 5. One-sentence Offer: I help {{avatar}} get {{dream_outcome}} in {{time_delay}} without {{effort_sacrifice}}."

## Blueprint 1: Lead Magnet Maker
**Goal:** Ship a magnet they feel stupid saying no to.
> **Prompt:** "Build a lead magnet:
> • Type: Guide, Checklist, or Pricing/Audit.
> • Name: 5 options (include numbers).
> • Delivery: PDF or Video.
> • CTA: 'What to do next' + Scarcity."

## Blueprint 2: Content Unit (Hook • Retain • Reward)
**Goal:** Warm audience content.
> **Prompt:** "Create 3 content units:
> • Hook: Headline (Conflict, Recency, or Curiosity).
> • Retain: 3 steps or 150-word story.
> • Reward: Explicit takeaway.
> • Soft CTA to {{lead_magnet}}."

## Blueprint 3: Warm Outreach (ACA)
**Goal:** Referral or Activation.
> **Prompt:** "Draft 3-touch warm outreach (Email/DM):
> • Touch 1: Acknowledge context + Compliment + Ask for referral.
> • Touch 2: Credible result + 'Taking 5 estimates this week'.
> • Touch 3: 'Are you still looking to update your {{kitchen}}?'"

## Blueprint 4: Cold Outreach
**Goal:** Introduction to Strangers.
> **Prompt:** "Create 3-step cold sequence:
> • Line 1: Personal proof/observation.
> • Line 2: Big Fast Value (Free Audit/Estimate).
> • Line 3: Micro-CTA (2 times available).
> • Follow-ups: Simple nudge."

## Blueprint 5: Paid Ads
**Goal:** Qualify and Convert.
> **Prompt:** "Build a Direct Response Ad:
> • Callout: {{geo}} {{avatar}}.
> • Body: Value Equation (Outcome, Proof, Speed, Ease).
> • Status Lift: 'Neighbors will be jealous'.
> • CTA: 1 Verb + 1 Destination."

## Blueprint 6: Landing Page Wireframe
**Goal:** Capture Contact Info.
> **Prompt:** "Produce Landing Page Copy:
> • Hero: Headline (Promise) + Form.
> • Proof Strip: Logos/Stats.
> • How it works: 3 Simple Steps.
> • CTA: Urgent/Scarcity."

## Blueprint 7: Referral Engine
**Goal:** Compounding Leads.
> **Prompt:** "Create Referral Program:
> • Trigger: On 'Wow' moment (project completion).
> • Script: 'Know anyone else looking to remodel?'
> • Incentive: $500 for you, $500 for them."

## Blueprint 12: CTA Generator
**Goal:** Action Now.
> **Prompt:** "Generate 10 CTAs:
> • 1 Verb + 1 Action + 1 Payoff.
> • Add 'Because' reason (Scarcity/Urgency).
> • Keep under 12 words."

## Blueprint 15: Metrics
**Goal:** Track Truth.
> **Prompt:** "Define benchmarks for: CTR, Opt-in %, App %, Show %, Close %, CAC."
